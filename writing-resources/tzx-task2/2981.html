Question

Some people think that, to learn about other countries, traveling abroad is   necessary while others think that is not necessary because TV and the internet can give the same information.


Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your knowledge and experience.

Write at least 250 words.

Sample Answer

People have different views on whether or not it's necessary to travel abroad in order to learn about foreign countries. Though I understand that TV and the internet can provide plenty of information, I don't think that it's entirely enough. 


On the one hand, learning from TV and the internet does teach you quite a bit about a foreign country. Television films and shows provide a window to the country, with documentary films being particularly informative. For instance, I remember watching a documentary about South Africa and learning a lot about the customs, culture, and way of life—not only from the film footage, but also from the narrator, who provided many extra details. The internet, too, contains a wealth of information on any country, including its history, geography, politics, economy and culture. It is even possible to digitally tour a country's streets by viewing them through online maps. 


These things, however, don't quite compare to actually visiting the country. When you visit a country, you're experiencing something yourself rather than being told or shown what it's like. This is important because there is a clear difference between second and first-hand experiences. First-hand experiences leave a much deeper impression, enabling you to more effectively learn and remember everything about a place. On top of that, visiting a country allows you to interact with the local people, who could teach you about their culture and relate their personal experiences. The cultural exchange exposes you to their true values and beliefs and clears up any misunderstandings. 


For these reasons, even though TV and the internet provide lots of information about other countries, I do think it's necessary to actually visit the countries to truly learn about them.
