Question

An increasing number of people are choosing to have cosmetic surgery in order to improve their appearance. 


Why do people have operations to change the way they look? 


Do you think this is a positive or negative development?


Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your knowledge and experience.

Write at least 250 words.

Sample Answer

In recent years, cosmetic surgery has become increasingly popular, as more people want to change their appearance. This essay will discuss the reasons why individuals choose to undergo such operations and argue that this is mostly a negative development.

 

One main reason is the growing influence of modern beauty standards. Social media, advertisements, and celebrities often promote an ideal image of beauty, which makes people feel pressured to look a certain way. For example, young people may see influencers online who have “perfect” features and then decide to have surgery to look more attractive. Another reason is personal confidence. Some people are unhappy with parts of their bodies, and they believe that surgery will improve their self-esteem. In some cases, surgery may also help people’s careers, especially in industries such as entertainment or fashion where appearance plays a big role.

 

However, I believe this trend has more disadvantages than benefits. Firstly, cosmetic surgery is risky and can lead to serious health problems if the operation goes wrong. Even when successful, patients may suffer from side effects or need more surgeries in the future. Secondly, it can create an unhealthy obsession with physical appearance. Instead of accepting natural beauty, people may always feel dissatisfied and want more changes. Finally, cosmetic surgery is expensive, so it increases social inequality, as only wealthy people can afford it, while others may go into debt to pay for it.

 

In conclusion, the popularity of cosmetic surgery is driven by modern beauty standards and the desire for greater self-confidence. While it may bring short-term satisfaction for some individuals, I think it is a negative development overall because of the health risks, psychological problems, and financial costs.
