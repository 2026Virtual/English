Question

Nowadays parents in many countries give their children more freedom than in the past.


Is this a positive or a negative trend?

Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your knowledge and experience.

Write at least 250 words.

Sample Answer

In recent decades, parenting styles in many societies have shifted to allow children more independence compared to previous generations. While some argue that this trend may lead to irresponsible behavior, I believe it is largely positive as it fosters essential life skills and emotional resilience when balanced with proper guidance.


One significant advantage of this approach is that it helps children develop problem-solving abilities and self-reliance. When allowed to make age-appropriate decisions—such as managing pocket money or choosing extracurricular activities—young people learn to analyze consequences and take responsibility. For instance, Scandinavian countries that emphasize independent play report higher levels of creativity and adaptability in children. Moreover, experiencing minor failures in a controlled environment, like forgetting homework, teaches valuable lessons without severe consequences, preparing them for adult challenges.


However, critics contend that excessive freedom may result in poor decision-making or safety risks, especially with modern dangers like internet misuse. While these concerns are valid, the solution lies not in restriction but in gradual empowerment with parental support. Studies show that children whose parents combine freedom with open communication—discussing online safety rather than banning devices—develop better judgment than those under strict control. The key is providing a scaffolding structure where freedom expands as children demonstrate maturity.


In conclusion, the trend toward increased childhood freedom is beneficial overall, cultivating capable and confident future adults. Parents should continue granting age-appropriate autonomy while maintaining emotional support and clear boundaries to ensure healthy development.
