Question

Some people think watching TV is bad for children in every way. Others think TV has positive effects on children development.


Discuss both these views and give your own opinion.


Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your knowledge and experience.

Write at least 250 words.

Sample Answer

Commonly, many adults are worried about the negative influences of watching television on young generations. And also, another group of persons holds an optimist thought towards this phenomenon. From my point of view, I believe that watching the tube can bring both merits and disadvantages to boys and girls, and the reasons will be presented below.


On the one hand, the priority con of watching the box is that children’s mental health might be adversely influenced by some TV programs since children can not select and distinguish what knowledge is suitable for them to watch. This means a considerably large amount of TV programs aimed at no specific audience, and the programs which contain pornography and violent content might be seen by young individuals. It will lead to the imitation of unlawful behaviors in the shows among young generations such as fighting, digging drugs, and rape. In China, for instance, there is no rating system to denote a TV series’ suitability for certain age groups. Chinese young generations are eligible to watch any bloody or violent programs without companions. This might explain why the crime rate of juvenile offenders in China are remaining the growth in recent decades. Therefore, it is important to establish a censorship system to guarantee the quality and value of tv programs for the young generations.


On the other hand, it is also undeniable that the stunning and colorful images on TV can dramatically stimulate kids’ interest in learning, and also it can help children to remember more information. A report in the Times showed, compared with traditional textbooks and lectures, visualizations of knowledge on TV can provide mixtures of information that substantially help individuals to possess an improvement in their memorizing of knowledge. Besides that, memories with images can last longer than literal information. In this case, educational and inspirational tv programs can be encouraged to be broadcast among kids. Because it seems that watching proper shows can bring plenty of benefits to children’s studying.


In conclusion, even though there is much controversy about whether children are beneficial from watching TV. I think it can be both harmful and advantageous to young generations. Hence, children must be watching educational and suitable tv shows instead of restricted-rated programs.
