Question

Nowadays, it is more convenient and easier for people to travel to other countries.


Is this a positive or negative development?

Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your knowledge and experience.

Write at least 250 words.

Sample Answer

It is true that it is becoming increasingly convenient for people to travel abroad to overseas  destinations. In my opinion, I strongly believe that this is an overall positive development for the following reasons.  


First of all, people acquire more intimate, in-depth knowledge of other countries and cultures from traveling abroad. By traveling to other countries, people are able to immerse themselves in the cultural diversity that exists in the world, which can help them to challenge their preconceptions and reduce prejudice. Moreover, traveling to other countries provides an excellent opportunity for people to broaden their horizons and learn new things, including history, culture, and perhaps even a foreign language. Finally, traveling enables people to reflect more deeply on themselves and the world around them, which can help to put their own lives into perspective. Thus, traveling can be seen as an inherently valuable educational experience that is both enriching and enlightening for tourists. 


In addition, the increasing popularity of tourism can help to stimulate the local economy. In fact, tourism has become the backbone for many countries around the world. The rising number of tourists creates numerous jobs in the tourist industry, including travel agents, tour guides, hotel staff, tour drivers, and so on. These jobs provide indispensable employment opportunities for local citizens and benefit local industries. Furthermore, the massive revenue generated from tourism can be used to improve the country's infrastructure and raise its standard of living, expanding access to essential goods and services. As a result, the country's image could also be boosted around the world, which may help to attract foreign investment. 


To conclude, I believe that increased overseas tourism is a positive development because it gives people the opportunity to learn more about the world and it boosts the economy of many countries.
