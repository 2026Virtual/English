学习商科还是历史

Question

Some people think that there could be more benefits to society if more people study business than history. 

To what extent do you agree or disagree?


Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your knowledge and experience.

Write at least 250 words.

Sample Answer

Some people argue that society would benefit more if more people studied business rather than history. While business knowledge is undeniably important, I believe that history also plays a crucial role in shaping a well-rounded society. In this essay, I will explain why both fields are valuable, but history should not be overlooked.


Business studies are often seen as more practical because they provide skills that can directly contribute to economic growth. For example, learning about management, finance, and marketing can help people start their own businesses or work in industries that drive the economy. In today’s fast-paced world, businesses need skilled workers who can innovate and adapt to global markets. Therefore, focusing on business studies could lead to job creation, higher productivity, and a better standard of living for society.


However, the study of history offers many advantages that are equally important. History helps us understand how societies have evolved over time, giving us insights into human behavior, political systems, and cultural values. By studying past events, we can learn from mistakes, avoid repeating them, and make informed choices in the future. For example, understanding the causes of wars or economic crises can help prevent similar problems from arising again. Additionally, history encourages critical thinking and a deeper understanding of the world, which is essential for making informed choices as citizens.


In conclusion, while business studies provide practical skills that contribute to economic success, history offers invaluable lessons that help us understand the present and shape the future. In my opinion, more people should study history to gain a deeper understanding of the world around them.
