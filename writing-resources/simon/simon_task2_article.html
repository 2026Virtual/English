1) Understand the task

•至少 250 words，40min

•大作文涵盖 universal topics（普遍话题：如 family\health\education\work），主要有 4 种类型

•4 个评分标准：专注于前三个最为重要，因为语法提升最慢；每个评分标准占据 25%的分值

oTask response 任务回应

oCoherence and Cohesion 连贯性与一致性

组织 Well organized、逻辑 logically written、易于理解 easy to follow

oLexical Resource 词汇

词汇这一点并不是说你用词高级复杂就对了，讲究的是用词的 variety，且要 precise。建议大家把握不准的单词别用。

我的考场 7 分文章大概单词拼写错误有 2-3 个，大家不用过于焦虑单词拼写。

oGrammatical range and accuracy 语法范围和准确性

2) Break the task into parts

250 words

•4 paragraphs

•about 13 sentences

oIntroduction: 2 sentences

不要写很长、很复杂的 intro，并不会对你的分数影响很大。如果想要高分，应该 focus 在中间的两个段落。

oTwo main paragraphs: 5 sentences each

oConclusion: 1 sentence

40 minutes

•Planning：10 minutes

Simon 非常强调打草稿的步骤，40 分钟的大作文 planning 就占了 10 分钟！

•Introduction：5 minutes

•Two main paragraphs：10 minutes for each

•Conclusion：5 minutes（包括检查时间）

2. Introductions

本节主要讲大作文开头 Introductions 怎么写

不断地使用同一个方法/模式在练习写 intro，这样才会越来越快，也写得越来越自信。（这一点我深有体会，每次我都是用 simon 的框架在写 intro 和 conclusion，这两部分我写得很快，而且不担心会出错。一开始的 paraphrase 可能会慢一点，但是多加练习就会熟练起来。）

1) 写作有四类题型

•Discussion

•Opinion

•Problem and solution

•Two-part question

2) Introduction 回答模式

Introduction 只需要写两句话（30-40 words），大家无需过于纠结于此。不同写作题型的 Introduction 有些微区别。

•第一句：introduce the topic.介绍文章的主题

•第二句：give a general answer.对这个问题给出一个非常笼统的回答（机考的话我一般会把这句话的框架写出来，具体的等中间两段写完再填充进去，因为我认为这句话就是给考官看我接下来要说的内容，所以这句话要根据 body paragraphs 来写。）

•不要在 introduction 里写太长，不然就是浪费时间，你不可能只靠 introduction 就得到高分！所以节省时间给后文吧！

3) Discussion

两种观点都要讲，然后给出自己的偏好

Prompt:

Some people think that it is more effective for students to study in groups, while others believe that it is better for them to study alone. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

Plan:

•Topic - study in groups or alone

•Answer - sometimes better alone, usually better in a group.

Answer:

•Topic - People have different views about the effectiveness of group study as opposed to working alone.

•Answer - While there are some benefits to studying independently, I believe that group work is usually more productive.

o需要在这句话中涵盖 both views and your own opinions

oThere are advantages to doing something

总结开头模板：People have different views about the … of A as opposed to B. While there are some benefits to A, I believe that B is usually more ….

4) Opinion: to what extent do you agree or disagree?(可以中立）

•不可以写 some people think（别人的观点），必须写我们自己的观点 our own opinion，因为现在题目问的是我们自己的想法。

Prompt:

Some people believe that unpaid community service should be a compulsory part of high school programs. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

Plan:

•Topic - community service for all teenagers

•Answer -我们有三种倾向的选择: agree, disagree, or balanced opinion (when possible)

Answer:

•Topic - It is sometimes argued that high school students should be made to do some work in their local communities.

•Answer

oAgree: I completely agree that this kind of scheme would be a good idea.

oDisagree: In my opinion, it would be wrong to force teenagers to do any kind of unsalaried work.

obalanced opinion: While I disagree with the idea of making such programs compulsory, I do believe that voluntary community service could benefit young people.

o因为 compulsory 是一个有强烈感情色彩的词，要么就是强制性的，要么是非强制的，很难有中间立场。因此采用此种观点：会对年轻人有好处。

总结开头模板：It is sometimes argued that sb should ….

1. I completely agree that … would be a good idea.

2. In my opinion, it would be wrong to ….

3. While I disagree with the idea of A, I do believe that B could benefit sb.

5) Problem and solution

Prompt:

Many criminals reoffend after they have been punished. Why do some people continue to commit crimes after they have been punished, and what measures can be taken to tackle this problem?

Plan:

•Topic - criminals reoffend

•Answer - several reasons, a variety of measures (governments, communities)

o不需要在此给出具体的 reasons 和 measures，那是 body 段的内容

Answer:

•Topic - It is true that punishments do not always deter criminals from committing more crimes.

•Answer - There are various reasons why offenders repeatedly break the law, but governments could certainly take steps to address this issue.

在开头无需给出解决措施，那个是 main 部分的内容了。

有意思的是，在这篇开头中写明了解决问题的主体是政府，在最开始就限定缩小了我们要讨论的范围。很多人可能纠结于教育、父母、社会环境…以至于迷失方向。且本题只有一个段落能让你讨论解决措施，如果在开头就限定主体的话，我认为是很巧妙定下了后文的框架。值得学习：只讨论某个主体所受的影响、解决方案……

6) Two-part question

Prompt:

As most people spend a major part of their adult life at work, job satisfaction is an important element of individual well-being. What factors contribute to job satisfaction? How realistic is the expectation of job satisfaction for all workers?

这里有两个独立的问号，所以要在 main body 里一段回答一个问号

Plan:

•Topic -job satisfaction

•Answer -several factors, unrealistic / impossible

Answer:

•Topic - Work plays a central role in our lives, and we would all like to feel fulfilled professionally.

•Answer - While a variety of factors may lead to job satisfaction, it would be unrealistic to expect everyone to be happy at work.

7) 总结

•简短的介绍更好

•5min 内写好 2 句话（topic+answer）

我想 Simon 在此想告诉我们的是，我们应该把重点放在后面的 main body paragraph 上，轻轻扫过 introduction 即可。

3. Main body paragraph

1) Answer methods

•2 main body paragraphs

•5 sentences in each

•90 to 100 words each

2) 中间段落有 2 种写作方式

•Firstly, Secondly, Finally

o适用于：advantages/disadvantages/problems/solutions

•Idea, Explain, Example

o适用于：One idea / a reason / an opinion

2 种写作方式，选其中之一即可。我一般都选用第二种方式，因为我认为这样才会有足够的空间让我 extend/解释我的观点。使用第一种的话，我也只会写两点，很少写三点。个人意见，仅供参考，目标分 7 分以上不要参考！因为我不了解！

3) Firstly, Secondly, Finally

Prompt:

Some people believe that unpaid community service should be a compulsory part of high school programs. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

Plan(3-minute):

•disagree for several reasons.

oschool timetable is full, no time for community service.

ostudents’ work in other subjects would be affected.

oteenagers might not want to do it (reluctant, no motivation)

当你头脑风暴结束后，要分清楚你现在所写的关键词是独立不同的点吗？还是其实都是同一个点，只是更详细地扩展开了。如果是前者，就是使用 Firstly, Secondly, Finally 结构。

Answer(4 sentences):

•disagree for several reasons.

oThere are several reasons why I would argue against having compulsory community service for secondary school students.

总起段落，说明我下面的句子主要是用于罗列 several reasons why

•school timetable is full, no time for community service.

oFirstly, the school curriculum is already full with important academic subjects, such as math, science and languages. For example, I remember having an extremely busy timetable when I was at high school, and it would not have been possible to add to it.

•students’ work in other subjects would be affected.

oSecondly, students’ performance in other subjects would be affected if valuable study time were taken by charity work or neighborhood improvement schemes.

蓝色高亮部分相当于把题目中的 unpaid community service 更具体化了！同理第一句里的 math, science and languages 也是把 other important academic subjects 具象化了，学起来！

•teenagers might not want to do it (reluctant, no motivation)

oFinally, I believe that teenage students would be reluctant to take part in any program of obligatory work, and this could lead to poor motivation and even bad behavior.

To what extent 类型的题目，一定要表达自己的观点，要在文章中明显给出这些信号，段落最后一句话 I believe that 就是一个很好的方式。

Firstly, Secondly, Finally 总结:

•写了五句话：主题句、Firstly（增加解释或者举一个例子）、Secondly、 Finally

•连接词 linking words 并不能帮助提高词汇分数，考官想看到的是主题词 topic vocabulary（连接词就是类似 Firstly…，它的作用就是让你的文章更清晰，让你的想法易于理解，这就够了）

主题词：

•compulsory community service、charity work or neighborhood improvement schemes、obligatory work

•school curriculum、academic subjects

•extremely busy timetable、valuable study time

•students’ performance

•reluctant to take part in

•poor motivation

4) Idea, Explain，Example

Prompt:

Some people believe that unpaid community service should be a compulsory part of high school programs. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

Plan (3-minute):

•voluntary (not compulsory) community service is positive.

•students more motivated if they can choose.

•gain work experience, self confidence, skills.

•good for CVs, career, university admissions, employers

Answer (4 sentences):

•voluntary (not compulsory) community service is positive.

oOn the other hand, the opportunity to do voluntary community service could be extremely positive for high school students.

总起段落，表明观点：有参加社区服务的机会是好事。

•students more motivated if they can choose.

oBy making these programs optional, schools would ensure that only motivated students took part.

•gain work experience, self confidence, skills.

oThese young people would gain valuable experience in an adult working environment, which could help to build their self confidence and enhance their skills.

•good for CVs, career, university admissions, employers

oHaving such experience and skills on their CVs could greatly improve school leavers’ career prospects. For example, a period of voluntary work experience might impress a university admissions officer or a future employer.

让社区服务成为可选项目→这样同学可以自愿参加→①获得工作经验、自信、技能②对简历有帮助、职业前景

Idea，Explain，Example 总结:

•写 5 句话：想法、解释（2-3 句话）、案例

•可以把 example 放在 explain 前面：idea、example、explain

主题词：

•Opportunity

•voluntary community service

•programs optional

•ensure that only motivated students took part.

•gain valuable experience in an adult working environment.

•build their self confidence and enhance their skills.

•school leavers’ career prospects.

•impress a university admissions officer or a future employer.

在这篇文章里，simon 第一个 main body paragraph 用的是 first/second/third，第二个用的是 idea, explain, example。同一篇文章里，两种方法可以交替使用。

4. Conclusion

1) Answer methods

•不要写任何“新”的东西（要贯彻全文的观点）repeat

•一句话就够了：重复/总结

2) Conclusion 技巧

将在 introduction 中给出的答案再 paraphrase 一下

3) Discussion（+opinion）

Prompt:

In many cities the use of video cameras in public places is being increased in order to reduce crime, but some people believe that these measures restrict our individual freedom.

Do the benefits of increased security outweigh the drawbacks?

Introduction:

It is true that video surveillance has become commonplace in many cities in recent years. While I understand that critics may see this as an invasion of privacy, I believe that there are more benefits than drawbacks.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, l would argue that the advantages of using video security systems in public places do outweigh the disadvantages.

总是使用 in conclusion 作为开头，句子里面没有什么新东西，只是单纯的在重复。

总结开头模板：It is true that A has become … in recent years. While I understand that … may see this as…, I believe that there are more benefits than drawbacks.

总结结尾模板：In conclusion, l would argue that the advantages of A do outweigh the disadvantages.

4) Opinion

Prompt:

Families who send their children to private schools should not be required to pay taxes that support the state education system. To what extent do you agree or disagree with this statement?

Introduction:

Some people believe that parents of children who attend private schools should not need to contribute to state schools through taxes. Personally, I completely disagree with this view.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, I do not believe that any financial concessions should be made for people who choose private education.

总结开头模板：Some people believe that … should…. Personally, I completely disagree with this view.

总结结尾模板：In conclusion, I do not believe that… should …

5) Problem and solution

Prompt:

In the developed world, average life expectancy is increasing. What problems will this cause for individuals and society? Suggest some measures that could be taken to reduce the impact of aging populations.

Introduction:

It is true that people in industrialized nations can expect to live longer than ever before. Although there will undoubtedly be some negative consequences of this trend, societies can take steps to mitigate these potential problems.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, various measures can be taken to tackle the problems that are certain to arise as the populations of countries grow older.

总结开头模板：It is true that …. Although there will undoubtedly be some negative consequences of this trend, … can take steps to mitigate these potential problems.

总结结尾模板：In conclusion, various measures can be taken to tackle the problems that are ….

6) 2-part question

Prompt:

There are many different types of music in the world today. Why do we need music? Is the traditional music of a country more important than the international music that is heard everywhere nowadays?

Introduction:

It is true that a rich variety of musical styles can be found around the world. Music is a vital part of all human cultures for a range of reasons, and I would argue that traditional music is more important than modern, international music.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, music is a necessary part of human existence, and I believe that traditional music should be given more importance than international music.

7) Conclusion 写作技巧总结

•只写一个句子就够了

•句子里不需要任何新的信息

•通过使用意译的方式来展示自己词汇的多样性

5. Planning

•Planning 大概花 10 分钟，你需要在 10 分钟内做以下三件事

oRead and understand the question

oPlan your essay structure (4 paragraphs)

oPlan ideas for the two main paragraphs (6 minutes)

Prompt: Some people think that it is more effective for students to study in groups, while others believe that it is better for them to study alone. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

1) Read and understand the question.

Some people think that it is more effective for students to study in groups, while others believe that it is better for them to study alone. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

看清楚题目很重要。首先要看清楚题目讨论的东西是什么、对应的主体是谁。很多人在这部分没有搞清楚就急匆匆往下想内容了，但是很有可能跑题。

2) Plan your essay structure (4 paragraphs)

•Introduction: topic + answer

ostudy in groups or alone, sometimes better alone, usually better in a group

•Benefits of studying alone

•Benefits of group study (my view)

•Conclusion: repeat answer

oboth have benefits, but l prefer group

题目说的是“有人觉得 xx 更好，有人觉得 xx 更好”，那么我们在计划框架时，就可以选择“一段写 A 好、一段写 B 好”，而不是 “一段写 A 不好、一段写 B 好”。因为我们要做到 task response，题目让我们做的是 Discuss both views，那就要根据题目本身来讨论！

3) Plan ideas for the two main paragraphs (6 minutes)

•头脑风暴：写下所有你想到的东西

•将 ideas 完善细节

•不断问自己 why（非常重要！！！！）然后写下来

•想出例子支持你的 ideas

•最终尝试把 ideas 联系在一起

建议大家在接下来的 6-10 课中，每次都在 plan ideas for the two main paragraphs 部分看完 simon 的头脑风暴和排序号后，自己跟着做一遍自己的版本，要有主动性~~

6. Opinion essay

•题目一般为 Do you agree or disagree?

o在开头一定要说 I agree/disagree OR in my opinion，直接回答问题，并且贯彻全文。观点要明确，不要说别人怎么样（比如 it’s agreed that…就不能用），不能只引用别人的观点。

o一定要说 I agree 或者 my 这类比较主观的词。

Prompt: The money spent by governments on space programs would be better spent on vital public services such as schools and hospitals. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

1) Read and understand the question

The money spent by governments on space programs would be better spent on vital public services such as schools and hospitals. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

主体这里说的是政府的钱，如果反对的话，那么大家可以考虑说 space programs 应该由私人资助，私人资助那就可以想到 Elon Musk，Space X 这个例子。

2) Plan your essay structure (4 paragraphs)

•Introduction: government spending agree -public services instead of space projects

•explain why 'space' spending should be stopped

•explain why public service spending is better

•Conclusion: spend on services that benefit us all

本文对比的对象是太空探索 vs 公共服务，那么结构就可以清晰分为一段反对 space，一段支持公共服务，结构和思路都比较简单清晰，在写作过程中也不容易出现车轱辘话讲来讲去的冗杂现象。

3) Plan ideas for the two main paragraphs (6 minutes)

•Why space spending should be stopped

o4waste of money when projects fail,2expensive (scientists, facilities, equipment)3,no benefit to normal people, politicians showing power, risk of accidents4, deaths e.g. 5Challenger space shuttle

•Why public service spending is better

o2cheaper e.g. doctors, teachers, police instead of astronauts, 3public services

impact on everyone,5reduce poverty, better quality of life,4we all use schools, hospitals, police, roads etc.

4) 正式写作

Introduction

Prompt: The money spent by governments on space programs would be better spent on vital public services such as schools and hospitals. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

Plan:

•Topic - government spending

•Answer - agree, public services instead of space projects

Answer:

•Topic - Governments in some countries spend large amounts of money on space exploration programs.

•Answer - I completely agree with the idea that these are a waste of money, and that the funds should be allocated to public services.

Main paragraphs

•why space spending should be stopped.

oThere are several reasons why space programs should be abandoned.

oFirstly, it is extremely expensive to train scientists and other staff involved with space missions, and facilities and equipment also come at a huge cost to the government.

oSecondly, these programs do not benefit normal people in our daily lives; they are simply vanity projects for politicians.

oFinally, many missions to space fail completely, and the smallest technological error can cost astronauts their lives.

原因①：训练相关科学家和工作人员很贵、设备也很贵

原因②：太空项目无法受益于普通人的日常生活

原因③：很多项目都失败了、小错误也会危机生命

•why public service spending is better.

oI believe that the money from space programs should go to vital public services instead.

oIt is much cheaper to train doctors, teachers, police, and other public service workers than it is to train astronauts or the scientists and engineers who work

on space exploration projects.

oFurthermore, public servants do jobs that have a positive impact on every member of society. For example, we all use schools, hospitals, and roads, and we all need the security that the police provide.

oIf governments reallocated the money spent on space travel and research, many thousands of people could be lifted out of poverty or given a better quality of life.

Conclusion

•In conclusion, my view is that governments should spend money on services that benefit all members of society, and it is wrong to waste resources on projects that don’t improve our everyday lives.

7. Discussion Essay

Prompt: Some people think that a sense of competition in children should be encouraged. Others believe that children who are taught to co-operate rather than compete become more useful adults. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion.

1) Read and understand the question.

这题的重点在于 what to，而不是对比两个媒介！

Answer:

•Topic - It is true that editors have to make difficult decisions about which news stories they broadcast or publish, and their choices are no doubt influenced by a variety of factors.

•Answer - In my opinion, we are exposed to too much bad news, and I would welcome a greater emphasis on good news.

Main paragraphs

•variety of factors that influence news editors

oEditors face a range of considerations when deciding what news stories to focus on.

oFirstly, I imagine that they have to consider whether viewers or readers will be interested enough to choose their television channel or their newspaper over competing providers.

oSecondly, news editors have a responsibility to inform the public about important events and issues, and they should therefore prioritize stories that are in the public interest.

oFinally, editors are probably under some pressure from the owners who employ them. For example, a newspaper owner might have particular political views that he or she wants to promote.

原因①：编辑应该考虑的是观众是否对他们的内容感兴趣；

原因②：编辑有责任让公众了解重要事件→应优先考虑符合公众利益的报道；

原因③：编辑可能会受到来自老板的一些压力。例如，雇主可能有自己想要宣传的政治观点（换个角度，从外部因素考虑。新闻和政治是永远连在一起的，因为政治人物需要靠媒体宣传而获得他们的选票，所以可以从这点切入。）。

•accustomed to bad news

oIt seems to me that people do become accustomed to negative news.

oWe are exposed on a daily basis to stories about war, crime, natural disasters and tragic human suffering around the world.

oI believe that such repeated exposure gradually desensitizes people, and we become more cynical about the world and more skeptical that we can do anything to change it.

oI would prefer to see more positive news stories, such as reports of the work of medical staff after a natural disaster, or the kindness of volunteers who help in their communities. This kind of news might inspire us all to lead better lives.

巧用 such、in this way、this、these、there、这些连词来连接起句子。
